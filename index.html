What is Smartschool+?

Smartschool+ is a tool for Smartschool, giving you extra control with database acces, personalized profiles, grade calculations, and interface customization. It's designed so you can better monitor results and give more control over your Smartschool expierence.

What can you do with Smartschool+?

  • Interface customization

    • Customizable Top Bar

      Change the background color of the Smartschool interface and enjoy a rainbow mode that cycles through colors.

    • Quick Shortcuts

      Enjoy quick shortcuts like a link to the "Planner" right on the top bar.

    • Profile Customization

      Personalize your profile by changing your username and profile picture.

  • User Search

    Find other Smartschool users by typing a word on your keyboard ("find"). You will see the full name of that person, their class, school and user ID. This is made possible by the database feature.

  • Database Access

    You get access to automatic databases of all students that get made just by using Smartschool+. You can get data about Smartschool users from this database. This includes names, classes, schools, and user ID's.

  • Grade calculation grid

    This calculates the total score of your grades, wich is a handy tool to keep track of your school results.

  • Personal AI coach

    A completely free personal AI coach to help students improve their results by pointing out their areas for improvement! Simply by a click of a button, it will look at all your results and coach you to get better results, or just motivate you! No accounts are needed to use the AI. The AI has auto-translation to any language, a long and detailed responses, and also a summarization feature, so you don't need to read that much! The AI will deliver responses ultra-fast if your school results have not changed since the last time you've used the AI.

  • Settings UI

    Adjust all of these features in the settings, including a slider to adjust the hue of the top bar and a checkbox to toggle between rainbow mode and slider mode.
